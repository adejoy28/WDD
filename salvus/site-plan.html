Purpose

The main purpose of my Salvus Website is get an online presence to showcase my works and other projects that I've participated in to the social world leading to more business and eventually more profit. To achieve this, the website will be exhibiting what I'm capable of to help solve a potiential clients problem which include the skills I have, the languages I use and other possible proferable solutions that can come from me.

The audience of my site will be people with the age of 16yrs and above and organizations. This includes individuals families, or organizations at large that wants a web development and related services.
